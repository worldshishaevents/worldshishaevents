Armin Shekari

Armin Shekari has achieved 1st Place in the World Shisha Dubai – Gulf Hookah Championship (2025). His performance stood out for exceptional flavour preparation, precise heat management, optimized airflow control, and professional service — earning him recognition as the champion of the event.

Armin’s signature techniques — now elevated and branded as “Hashed” — represent a fresh, refined, and innovative identity in the world of professional hookah performance.

A brands new home for the region's largest shisha show

After three wonderful years at Dubai Festival City, World Shisha Show is moving to the Dubai World Trade Centre, one of the world's most prestigious exhibition venues.

Centrally located with easy plenty of parking and it's own dedicated metro station, Dubai World Trade Centre provides a larger space to accommodate the event, enabling us to welcome a higher number of visitors and exhibitors from around the world.
